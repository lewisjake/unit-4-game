Crystal Collector!

You will be givien a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the fame bu matching your total score to the random number that was selected for you. You lose the game if your total score exceeds the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the random number, and the values of each crystal.
